Welcome to my developer blog!

Here, I aim to share my experiences as a full stack developer, and the lessons I've learned along the way. I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of different projects, from developing websites and single page applications, to building test automation frameworks and writing company UX guidelines.

Through personal anecdotes I want to provide a glimpse into the life of a professional developer, as well as the technologies I am the most excited about. I hope you'll find valuable insights and inspiration in these pages, especially if you are just starting out in development!
